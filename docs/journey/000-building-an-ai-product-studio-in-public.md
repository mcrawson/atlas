# Building an AI Product Studio in Public

**Journey Post #0 | The Beginning**
**Date:** 2026-03-15
**Author:** Amp

---

What if you could have an idea and get a sellable product?

Not a prototype. Not a "proof of concept." Not code that technically works but looks like a developer made it (because one did).

An actual product. Something you could put on Etsy, the App Store, or Amazon. Something a customer would pay for.

That's what we're building. And we're doing it in public.

---

## What is ATLAS?

**ATLAS** stands for Automated Thinking, Learning & Advisory System.

The fancy name aside, here's what it actually is:

> **ATLAS is a product studio that combines human creativity with ethical AI to build transformative solutions.**

You bring the ideas. ATLAS provides the AI agents that analyze, design, build, and promote.

Together, you create products that matter.

---

## Why Build This?

Because the current state of AI tools is frustrating.

You can use ChatGPT to write code. You can use Midjourney to make images. You can use a dozen different tools to do a dozen different things.

But none of them *finish the job*.

You still have to:
- Figure out if your idea is any good
- Design the thing yourself
- Stitch together code from different prompts
- Make it actually look sellable
- Handle deployment
- Write marketing copy

AI does the middle work. You still do everything else.

**ATLAS is different.**

ATLAS handles the entire pipeline:

1. **Idea Chat** — Discuss and refine your idea
2. **Business Analysis** — Is this worth building? Kill bad ideas early.
3. **Mockup** — See what you're getting before committing
4. **Build** — Specialized AI builders for each product type
5. **QC** — Quality control at every stage
6. **Deploy** — Push to marketplaces
7. **Promote** — Marketing content to sell it

You're in control at every checkpoint. AI does the heavy lifting. But you decide what gets built and when.

---

## Who Is This For?

Creators who have ideas but not time.

- **Entrepreneurs** who want to test product ideas quickly
- **Side hustlers** who need passive income products
- **Creators** who can envision but can't code
- **Developers** who can code but can't design

If you've ever thought "I wish I could just describe what I want and have it exist," ATLAS is for you.

---

## Why Build in Public?

Three reasons:

### 1. Accountability
It's easy to abandon projects in private. Harder when people are watching.

### 2. Learning
We're going to make mistakes. Documenting them helps others avoid the same traps.

### 3. Trust
If you're going to use AI to build products, you should see how the sausage gets made. No black boxes.

We'll share everything:
- The wins
- The failures (there will be many)
- The technical decisions
- The pivots
- The actual code

---

## What Can You Build With ATLAS?

When it's done:

| Category | Products | Marketplace |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| **Printables** | Planners, recipe cards, worksheets, journals | Etsy |
| **Documents** | Books, guides, manuals, ebooks | Amazon KDP |
| **Web** | Landing pages, dashboards, simple web apps | Vercel, custom hosting |
| **Mobile** | iOS and Android apps | App Store, Google Play |

Each product type has a specialized builder that knows exactly how to create sellable output for that category.

## Where Are We Now?

We're rebuilding.

ATLAS v1 and v2 produced *code*, not *products*. The output was technically correct but completely unsellable. A planner that was just HTML. Recipe cards that were just text.

So we went back to the drawing board. Defined the mission. Redesigned the entire workflow.

**Current Progress:**

| Phase | Status | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| Phase 0 | ✅ Complete | Defined mission and process |
| Phase 1 | ✅ Complete | Foundation reset (cleaned up codebase) |
| Phase 2 | 🔜 Next | Analyst agent (business intelligence) |
| Phase 3 | Planned | Specialized builders |
| Phase 4 | Planned | QC integration |
