# ATLAS Mission

> **ATLAS is a product studio that combines human creativity with ethical AI to build transformative solutions for our clients and the public.**

---

## What This Means

| Word | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Product studio** | We make finished, sellable products. Not demos. Not prototypes. Real products people pay for. |
| **Human creativity** | You bring the ideas, vision, and final judgment. AI is a tool, not a replacement. |
| **Ethical AI** | We build responsibly. Quality over speed. No shortcuts. No deception. |
| **Transformative solutions** | Products that solve real problems. That make a difference. That matter. |
| **Clients and the public** | For paying customers AND for broader impact. |

---

## For Agents

Every agent in ATLAS must:

1. **Know this mission** - It's the foundation of all work
2. **Tie decisions back to it** - "Does this serve the mission?"
3. **Prioritize quality** - Ethical AI means no shortcuts
4. **Support human creativity** - Enhance, don't replace
5. **Build transformative solutions** - Not just "working" products, but products that matter

---

## The Filter

Before completing any work, ask:

- Does this align with the mission?
- Is this a product someone would pay for?
- Did we build this ethically (no shortcuts, no deception)?
- Does this solve a real problem?
- Would we be proud to put our name on this?

If the answer to any of these is "no" - the work isn't done.

## For Every Project

- On screen when you use ATLAS
- In every decision we make
