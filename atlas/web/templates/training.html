How to Train Your Local ATLAS

1

Collect Data

Use ATLAS normally. Every approved agent output becomes training data.

2

Export Data

Once you have 100+ examples, export as JSONL format.

3

Fine-tune

Use tools like mlx_lm, axolotl, or unsloth to fine-tune a base model.

4

Deploy to Ollama

Convert your model to GGUF and add to Ollama.

5

Update Config

Configure ATLAS to route tasks to your local model first.
